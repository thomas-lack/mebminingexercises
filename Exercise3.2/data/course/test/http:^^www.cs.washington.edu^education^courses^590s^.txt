***** Preliminaries *****
If you're not already on the uw-systems mailing list, you need to be, because
that's where various crucial bits of information (e.g., ``this week's seminar
is cancelled'') will be sent. To get yourself on this list, send mail to uw-
systems-request with the line subscribe uw-systems in the message body.
