Since you are here, maybe you'd like to know a little about me? I doubt it.
Anyway, currently I'm a Master of Engineering student in the Department of
Computer_Science at Cornell_University. Cornell is located at Ithaca, central
New York, a gorgeous place to live except in winter. Last year, I received my
M.Sc in Applied Physics from New_Jersey_Institute_of_Technology, Newark, New
