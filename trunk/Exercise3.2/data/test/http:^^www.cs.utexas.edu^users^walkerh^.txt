A regular, tenured member of the Grinnell College faculty, Professor Walker
visits The University of Texas at Austin periodically to teach and for various
professional activities. Formal appointments to the Computer sciences
department have been as follows:
    * 1988-1989 academic year
    * Summers 1990, 1991, 1992, 1993
    * Fall, 1995
More complete information is available from Professor Walker's home page at
Grinnell College: http://www.math.grin.edu/~walker.
